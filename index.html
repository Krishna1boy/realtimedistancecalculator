How to use:

  • Click START to begin tracking your movement
  • The app will use your device's GPS to sense speed
  • Distance is calculated in real-time using: Distance = Speed Ã— Time
  • Click STOP to pause tracking
  • Click RESET to clear all data

Prerequisites for REAL Usage:

  • GPS-enabled device: Smartphone or tablet with actual GPS chip (NOT laptops)
  • Outdoor location: Clear sky view for best GPS signal
  • Physical movement: You must actually move (walk, run, bike, drive)
  • Location permission: Allow location access when prompted

Why laptops don't work: Laptops use WiFi/IP-based location (very inaccurate, no GPS chip). They can't detect speed or precise movement. Use a smartphone/tablet outdoors for real tracking!

Demo Mode: If you're on a laptop, the app will offer a simulation mode for testing the interface (not real GPS tracking).
